Our Story

Zippy's Slime started in 2018 when our founder, Zoe "Zippy" Patterson, was experimenting with homemade slime recipes in her kitchen. What began as a fun hobby quickly turned into a passion for creating the perfect slime texture.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of slime to bring joy, relieve stress, and spark creativity. Our mission is to create high-quality, safe, and endlessly fun slime products that make people of all ages smile.

Our Process

Each batch of Zippy's Slime is handcrafted with care using premium ingredients. We test every texture for the perfect stretch, squish, and bubble-pop potential before it reaches you!
